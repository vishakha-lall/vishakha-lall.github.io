Exciting Adventures at Work

Automating Movement of Inventories in the Warehouse

Flipkart (e-Commerce)

When warehouses are operated by robots, software engineers program the movement of inventories in the warehouse for picking, packing and shipping! Also, remember to take the most optimised path ;)

An intelligent solution (to find the inventory closest to our robot friend, move to the destination location by predicting frequency of the inventory's customer reservation) built in Java platform services.

Smart Stock Take in the Warehouse

Flipkart (e-Commerce)

Stock take validate the accuracy of inventory in the shelves against those in the database systems. But how can we predict the shelves to be stock taken? How can we randomize the shelf selection activity without blocking customer orders? How can we ensure that operations/robots travel minimum distance between shelves?

A complete Java platform service to generate which shelves are due for stock take activity.

Ranking Engine

Fidelity Investments

Can we really analyse our fund performance without comparing to several others (Fidelity and non Fidelity) on multiple parameters?

Ranking engine is an amalgamation of clustering algorithms to classify mutual funds into complicated categories (beyond the usual metrics) and a Java service that ranks these funds based on their performance in these categories.
